## Description: <br>
Helps solopreneurs identify, design, test, and maintain no-code automation workflows across business tools. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users and small-business operators use this skill to find repetitive tasks, choose no-code automation tools, map triggers and actions, test workflows, and maintain automations over time. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Automation workflows can grant broad access to third-party business tools through OAuth permissions. <br>
Mitigation: Review app permissions before connecting services and use the narrowest OAuth scopes available. <br>
Risk: Untested automations can change customer data, send messages, publish content, or process payments incorrectly. <br>
Mitigation: Test with non-sensitive data first and keep manual approval for payments, public posts, customer-impacting messages, or bulk data changes. <br>
